Web UX/UI

Luminary

A wellness platform redesigned for conversion — and desire.

Luminary had a great product and a website costing them customers. The checkout funnel was broken in three places, mobile was an afterthought, and the visual language didn't match their premium positioning.

The redesign consolidated 11 navigation paths into one intuitive journey. A new design system established consistency across 40+ product templates. Post-launch: session duration up 38%, cart abandonment down significantly.

Web UX/UI

Noor Financial

Banking made calm. Finance made legible.

Financial products carry inherent anxiety. Noor's existing platform amplified it — dense dashboards, unclear transaction flows, and an onboarding sequence seeing 60% drop-off before verification.

The redesign began with a full UX audit and 12 hours of moderated user sessions. The result: a stripped-back interface that surfaces what matters and guides new users through onboarding in under four minutes.

Mobile App UX/UI

Pockigo

Travel planning without the cognitive tax.

Planning a trip should feel like anticipation, not administration. Pockigo holds the full journey — itineraries, maps, bookings, notes — in one frictionless space.

Research with 24 frequent travellers mapped the key failure points of existing apps: information overload, poor offline performance, and confusing day-by-day navigation. The final design uses card-based architecture to keep users oriented whether they're at a gate or in the middle of a city.

Brand Identity

Velora Stonecraft

Identity as permanent as the material it represents.

Velora is a luxury natural stone brand at the highest tier of the residential and commercial materials market. The brief: build a visual identity that commands the same authority as the stone itself — enduring, rare, and undeniably premium.

The identity draws from the tension between mass and refinement — a geometric mark derived from natural fracture lines, a custom-adapted type system, and a three-colour palette extracted directly from the brand's hero stone. The result reads as confidently in a flagship showroom as on a billboard.
