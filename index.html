I offer UX/UI Design (wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, micro-animations) and Brand Identity (logo design, social media graphics, infographics, custom illustrations). Both are available as standalone or combined engagements.

I follow a Double Diamond process: Discover → Define → Design → Deliver. I keep you involved at every stage — from research and wireframes to final handoff with specs and a style guide.

Brand identity projects typically take 2–3 weeks. UX/UI projects for a website or app usually run 4–8 weeks from kickoff to final delivery. I always agree a clear timeline before we begin.
